Banned items


  • The one ring (Electro-Magic Tools)


    • Due to some player abuse, this led to wild withers and corrupted progression because of the warp amount


  • Fancy workbenches (Bibliocraft, BiblioWoods: Biome O' Plenty Edition, BiblioWoods: Forestry Edition and BiblioWoods: Natura Edition)


    • These workbenches are known to allow some duplication bugs


  • Fluid export bus (ExtraCells2)

    • Fluid AE through EC2 is laggy, bugged and can lead to easy fluid duplication. It may eventually removed in a future version of the ModPack.


  • Fluid import bus (ExtraCells2)

    • Fluid AE through EC2 is laggy, bugged and can lead to easy fluid duplication. It may eventually removed in a future version of the ModPack.


  • Fluid storage bus (ExtraCells2)

    • Fluid AE through EC2 is laggy, bugged and can lead to easy fluid duplication. It may eventually removed in a future version of the ModPack.


  • Oredictionnary Export Bus (ExtraCells2)

    • The oredictionnary export bus from EC2 is laggy, and it has a native AE2 alternative called Oredict Filter card. Please use this instead.
