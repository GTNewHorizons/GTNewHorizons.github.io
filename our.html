Indigenous Modifications

Short description of our mods and configuration files.


What makes our modpack different from others?

We have our own custom builds of many mods including GregTech. Hundreds of MineTweaker scripts to change the recipes, tons of quests and finally our own indigenous modifications! Some of them are secret to players, feel free to find out what they do. Other modifications are just pure APIs. Both client and server configuration files have been adjusted to make the playtrough feel even more balanced/hardcore.

GT New Horizons Core Mod

This indigenous modification further extends GregTech into new tiers and adds many new items to the game, making the crafting even harder. For instance thanks to this modification you can enjoy the GregTech like crafing of Applied Energistics 2 system.

Better Questing Mod

Tiered Questing System with over 500 Quests!!!

Quests will allow you to explore our modpack with ease and if u complete enough of them, you will notice that the rewards get better.

Some of the quest even allow you to get unique loot like GregTech ZPM or Witchery Infinity Egg, but this is going to take way more time.




  • YAMCore

    Yet Another Modding Core, yeah kind of neat name. Does just what the name implies.

  • Block Limiter

    Our own version of block usage limiting methods, further extended into item limiter.

  • GTTweaker

    This addon allows to customize GregTech recipes via MineTweake, obvious isn't it?
